## Vulture Safe Zones – Tamil Nadu (2025)

**Context**
- Tamil Nadu to establish Vulture Safe Zones (VSZs) following Madras HC proceedings.

**Key Facts (Prelims)**
- First VSZ: Moyar River Valley [ Moyar Rivers is west to east flowing, it is tributory of Bhavani River]
- Location: Nilgiris Biosphere Reserve
- Threat: Veterinary NSAIDs (Diclofenac)
- Authority: TN Forest Department

**Why it matters (Mains GS-III)**
- Addresses >95% decline in vulture population.
- Improves public health via safe carcass disposal.
- Example of state-led biodiversity governance.

**Governance Angle**
- Judicial intervention via PIL.
- Field-level monitoring committee.
- Inter-district coordination.

**One-line Mains Use**
> Tamil Nadu’s Vulture Safe Zones (2025) illustrate effective state-led species conservation backed by judicial oversight.
